# tokblok Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 26 April 2026**

tokblok ("we", "the app") is a productivity tool that helps you block or limit your own use of selected apps. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## TL;DR

- tokblok runs entirely on your device. We do not operate any backend server.
- We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.
- The only data that leaves your device is purchase information you submit to Google Play (on Android) or the App Store (on iOS) when you buy tokblok Premium.

## What data tokblok handles

All of the following is stored **only on your device** in a local SQLite database. None of it is uploaded to any server we control.

- **Your blocking policies** — the names you give them, which apps they cover, and the time-based or allowance-based conditions you configure.
- **App usage on your device** — for allowance-based policies, tokblok records how long covered apps have been in the foreground. This data never leaves your device and is used only to compute remaining allowance.
- **Your app settings** — including the changes-delay value, allowance-reset time, and notification preferences.
- **Premium entitlement state** — whether you have an active tokblok Premium subscription or lifetime purchase, and (where the platform reports it) when it expires.

We do not collect:

- Your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, calendar, messages, or files.
- Crash reports or analytics.
- Any device identifier we control.

## Permissions tokblok requests

- **Usage access (Android)** — so tokblok can see which app is currently in the foreground, in order to enforce blocking and track allowance time. This is processed entirely on-device.
- **Accessibility service / overlay permissions (Android, where applicable)** — so tokblok can intercept launches of blocked apps and display the block banner.
- **Notifications** — so tokblok can warn you when an allowance is running low.

## Purchases

tokblok offers an in-app purchase ("tokblok Premium") via Google Play Billing on Android and the App Store on iOS. When you make a purchase:

- Google Play (or Apple) handles the payment. tokblok never sees your payment-card details.
- tokblok receives a signed receipt from Google or Apple confirming the purchase, which is stored locally on your device to remember your entitlement.
- Purchase data is governed by [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and, on iOS, [Apple's privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Data sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. The only outbound data flow is the purchase request you initiate to Google Play or the App Store.

## Data retention and deletion

Because tokblok stores all data locally:

- **Uninstalling the app** removes all tokblok data from your device.
- You can also clear data via your operating system's app settings.
- There is no server-side data for us to delete because we do not operate a server.

## Security

Your tokblok data is stored in your device's per-app sandbox, which the operating system isolates from other apps. We do not transmit your data over the network.

## Children's privacy

tokblok is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and (where reasonable) surface the change in the app.
